Welcome to my little corner of the internet. I’m a **developer** and **student** who loves technology, videogames, and building things that live online.

## Who am I?

My name is Andrés, and for as long as I can remember I’ve been curious about how things work — from taking toys apart as a kid to dissecting other people’s code at 2 AM. I’m currently studying engineering while building projects that push me to learn something new every day.

When I’m not coding, I’m probably in some virtual world exploring maps, finishing quests, or competing online. Games aren’t just a hobby; they’ve taught me problem‑solving, teamwork, and, above all, not to give up after attempt #847.

## What I do

- **Web development** — I love building modern web experiences with tools like Astro, React, TypeScript, and the broader JavaScript ecosystem.
- **Open Source** — I believe in the power of open source. You can find my projects on [GitHub](https://github.com/0xdres/).
- **Continuous learning** — I’m always exploring new tools, frameworks, and concepts. Tech moves fast, and I enjoy keeping up.

## Gaming

Videogames are part of my DNA. From epic RPGs to competitive shooters, I enjoy almost any genre. It’s where creativity, strategy, and pure fun all meet.

## This blog

**Devosfera** started as a place to document my journey as a developer, share what I learn, and connect with people who share the same passions. Here you’ll find posts about development, technology, and the occasional late‑night thought.

If you’d like to connect, you can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/0xdres/), [X](https://x.com/avdyen), or [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/avdyan/). I’m always happy to meet cool people from the community.
